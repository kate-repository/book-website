# Project Brief for Codex: Pre-Sale Website for the Book

## 1. Project Goal

Create a professional one-page sales website for a Polish-language book about questioning the traditional university path. The website will be used for **pre-sale**, not for full book delivery yet.

The page must convince visitors who are uncertain about university, parents of teenagers, and young adults who are searching for phrases like:

- "czy warto iść na studia"
- "co studiować"
- "jakie studia wybrać"
- "nie wiem co studiować"
- "czy studia mają sens"
- "alternatywy dla studiów"
- "studia czy praca"
- "czy iść na studia bez planu"
- "książka dla maturzysty"
- "co po maturze"

The core message: **do not go to university blindly just because family, school, or society expects it. First, check whether it makes practical, financial, and personal sense.**

The website should support a pre-sale flow using Payhip. The payment itself will happen outside the website. The site only needs to send users to Payhip through a clear button.

---

## 2. Book Positioning

### Main positioning

This is a strong, opinionated book for people who are not sure whether university is the right path.

It is not a neutral education guide. It is a direct challenge to the belief that university automatically gives a better future.

### Suggested positioning statement

> Książka dla młodych ludzi i rodziców, którzy chcą podjąć świadomą decyzję, zanim wydadzą lata życia i duże pieniądze na studia bez konkretnego planu.

### Tone

The tone should be:

- strong
- clear
- direct
- practical
- slightly provocative
- serious but not academic
- personal and credible
- not childish
- not corporate
- not overly soft

The author’s position is conservative and critical of most university paths, especially when people study without a clear plan, practical skills, or financial awareness. The website should not constantly apologize for this view. One sentence can clarify that the book is not against every form of education, but the main tone should stay firm.

### What the book is against

- blindly going to university
- choosing studies because parents expect it
- believing that a diploma automatically means safety
- paying for education without checking practical value
- wasting years without building real skills
- choosing studies because someone does not know what else to do

### What the book is for

- practical skills
- personal responsibility
- financial awareness
- independent thinking
- alternative paths
- real experience
- making a conscious decision before university

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary audience

1. **Teenagers / young adults before university**
   - They search for “what should I study?” or “I don’t know what to study.”
   - They feel pressure from parents, school, or society.
   - They may believe university is the default path but are not sure.

2. **Parents of teenagers**
   - They want a better future for their child.
   - They may assume university is the safest option.
   - They may be paying or planning to pay for education.
   - They need to think about whether the cost and time are justified.

3. **People who already started or finished university and feel disappointed**
   - They may identify with the author’s experience.
   - They may be interested in the book because it validates doubts they already have.

### Search intent to capture

The best visitor is not necessarily someone who already types the book title. The best visitor may be someone searching:

- What should I study?
- What university should I choose?
- Is university worth it?
- What if I don’t know what to study?
- Alternatives to university.
- What to do after high school?

These people are valuable because they are already uncertain. The page should meet them at the moment of doubt and offer a different perspective.

## 6. Visual Design Direction

### Overall feeling

The website should feel like a serious, strong, modern book campaign — not like a school brochure.

### Avoid

- childish graphics
- stock photos of happy students with laptops
- university-style blue corporate design
- too much pastel softness
- generic education icons everywhere
- cluttered layout
- academic seriousness that feels boring

### Recommended style

- clean layout
- strong typography
- lots of whitespace
- slightly editorial/book-like feeling
- manifesto-style sections
- calm but confident
- premium but simple

### Suggested color palette

Option 1: serious and strong

- Background: warm off-white / cream (#F8F3EA)
- Text: near-black (#171717)
- Accent: deep red / burgundy (#8B1E1E)
- Secondary accent: muted gold (#B88A44)

Option 2: modern editorial

- Background: ivory (#FAF7F0)
- Text: charcoal (#1E1E1E)
- Accent: dark navy (#16213E)
- Secondary accent: copper (#A45C40)

Option 3: bold manifesto

- Background: white (#FFFFFF)
- Text: black (#000000)
- Accent: dark red (#9E1B1B)
- Highlight background: light beige (#EFE4D3)

### Typography

Use fonts that support Polish characters.

Recommended pairings:

- Headings: Playfair Display, Merriweather, Libre Baskerville, or Lora
- Body: Inter, Source Sans 3, Open Sans, or system sans-serif

The design should be readable on mobile. The page must not use tiny font sizes.

### Layout

- Mobile-first
- One main column for text
- Max content width around 1100–1200px
- Text blocks max width around 700–800px
- Large hero section
- Repeated CTA buttons
- Sticky CTA button on mobile can be considered
- FAQ accordion
- Clear preview section

## 8. Payhip Pre-Sale Flow

The website should not process payment. Payhip will process payment.

### Flow

1. User reads the page.
2. User clicks “Zamów w przedsprzedaży.”
3. User goes to Payhip product checkout.
4. User pays.
5. User receives a placeholder PDF or confirmation file.
6. When final book is ready, final file is uploaded/replaced in Payhip.
7. Buyers receive access to the final book according to Payhip settings.

### Placeholder file content

Create a placeholder PDF for Payhip with text similar to:

> Dziękuję za zakup książki w przedsprzedaży.
>
> To jest przedsprzedaż. Pełna wersja książki zostanie wysłana po premierze.
>
> Planowana data premiery: [INSERT DATE]
>
> Jeśli masz pytania, napisz na: [INSERT EMAIL]

### Important pre-sale wording

Use this on the website:

> To jest przedsprzedaż. Kupując teraz, otrzymujesz dostęp do dostępnego fragmentu i gwarancję otrzymania pełnej wersji książki po premierze.

Do not hide the fact that the book is not fully delivered immediately.

## 10. Advertising Strategy

### Best advertising angle

Target people who are actively uncertain about university.

The ad should not only target “book about university.” It should target doubt.

### Possible Google Ads keywords

Use phrase match and exact match variants around:

- "co studiować"
- "nie wiem co studiować"
- "jakie studia wybrać"
- "czy warto iść na studia"
- "czy studia mają sens"
- "co po maturze"
- "alternatywy dla studiów"
- "studia czy praca"
- "studia bez planu"
- "czy muszę iść na studia"
- "najlepsze kierunki studiów"
- "kierunek studiów jak wybrać"

### Important advertising insight

Someone searching “what should I study?” may already be unsure. The advertisement can interrupt the default assumption and ask:

> A co jeśli najpierw warto zapytać, czy studia w ogóle mają sens?

This can attract exactly the right audience.

### Sample ad headlines

- Nie wiesz, co studiować?
- Zanim wybierzesz studia
- Czy studia mają sens?
- Nie idź na studia bez planu
- Książka dla maturzystów
- Studia to nie plan na życie
- Co zamiast studiów?

### Sample ad descriptions

- Zanim wydasz lata życia i pieniądze na studia, sprawdź, czy to naprawdę Twoja droga.
- Książka dla młodych ludzi i rodziców, którzy chcą podjąć świadomą decyzję.
- Przeczytaj fragment i zamów w przedsprzedaży.
- Nie wybieraj studiów tylko dlatego, że wszyscy mówią, że musisz.

### Negative keywords to consider

To avoid wasting money, consider excluding searches that are not relevant:

- praca licencjacka
- praca magisterska
- uczelnia rekrutacja
- ranking uczelni
- plan zajęć
- stypendium
- akademik
- zaoczne konkretna uczelnia
- medycyna rekrutacja
- prawo rekrutacja

Do not exclude too aggressively at the beginning. First check search term reports after ads run.

### Landing page message for ads

For Google Ads traffic, the top of the page should match the search intent:

If user searches “nie wiem co studiować”, the page should say near the top:

> Nie wiesz, co studiować? Może najpierw warto sprawdzić, czy studia są właściwą decyzją.

This can be a secondary hero line or a dedicated section near the top.

## 11. Conversion Tips

### Repeat the CTA

Add “Zamów w przedsprzedaży” button after:

- hero
- problem section
- preview section
- pre-sale offer section
- final section

### Make buying feel safe

Explain:

- what happens after payment
- when the book will arrive
- what format it will be
- whether refund is possible
- who to contact

### Avoid weak language

Avoid:

- “Może warto się zastanowić...”
- “Niektóre studia czasami mogą nie być idealne...”
- “Każda droga jest dobra...”

Use stronger language:

- “Studia bez planu mogą być kosztowną pomyłką.”
- “Dyplom nie jest planem na życie.”
- “Nie wybieraj kilku lat życia pod presją.”

### Social proof

If available later, add:

- early reader quotes
- testimonials from parents
- testimonials from young adults
- number of pre-orders
- media mention, if any

For now, if there are no testimonials, do not fake them.

## 18. Final Conversion Message

The emotional core of the website should be:

> Zanim wydasz kilka lat życia i duże pieniądze na studia, zatrzymaj się i sprawdź, czy to naprawdę Twoja droga — czy tylko decyzja podjęta pod presją rodziny, szkoły i społeczeństwa.

This message should guide the full design, copy, SEO, and advertising strategy.

## 19. Definition of Done

The website is ready when:

- It works on mobile and desktop.
- All CTA buttons link correctly to Payhip.
- The pre-sale status is clear.
- The preview section is visible and easy to access.
- SEO title and meta description are set.
- Open Graph image is set.
- Legal pages exist.
- Contact email is visible.
- FAQ answers common objections.
- Page loads fast.
- It is visually consistent with a serious, strong, slightly provocative book.
- The page does not look like a generic school/university website.
